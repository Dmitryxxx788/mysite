Lego Spider-Man

Spider-Man first appeared in Marvel Comics’ Amazing Fantasy in 1962. The following year, he was given his own comic-book series, The Amazing Spider-Man. This was soon Marvel's top-selling title, and the character became a cultural icon. In 1972, Spider-Man’s popularity was used to raise the profile of other superheroes and villains with the Marvel Team Up series of comics, in which the young web-slinger was paired with a different character every issue.

Lego Black Panther

Black Panther made his debut in 1966’s Fantastic Four comic. After subsequent guest appearances with other characters, his location moved from his African homeland of Wakanda to New York, where he teamed up with fellow superheroes, The Avengers.In 2018, Black Panther appeared in his first self-titled, movie. The plot followed T’Challa as he returned to his homeland of Wakanda following the death of his father, the king. T’Challa inherits the throne – and the role of Black Panther, the masked superhero protector of his people. T'Challa gained superhuman powers from a Wakandan herb that instilled in him greatly enhanced abilities of speed, agility and strength. In addition, he wears an indestructible Vibranium-lined outfit that gives him even greater power and resilience.
